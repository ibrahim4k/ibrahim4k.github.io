Program: Toronto Metropolitan University - Business Technology Management (BComm)

This program is Canada's largest school dedicated to business management and communication technologies. It combines business and tech skills to teach students how to manage IT projects and align technology with business goals. Graduates can pursue roles in various careers such as IT consulting, business analysis, tech management, and more.

ITM 780: Web Design and Management
Intro to web design, HTML5, CSS3, and web standards.
RTA 928: Introduction to Game Design
